At CleanRobotics, We Are Working to Make Recycling Smarter

Automatic Sorting

TrashBot automatically sorts recyclable materials from landfill waste, so you don't have to.

Instaneous Audits

TrashBot keeps track of all items that are thrown away, so that facilities managers can have up-to-date data on their waste generation and makeup.

Fullness Indication

TrashBot lets you know when it's full, so that your custodians can spend their time doing more productive things.
